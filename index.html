My background

Ever since I was little, I’ve always loved building things for others. First it was Lego, then it was computers, where I discovered assembling something was even more fun than playing with it.

My career’s also been about building. I got my first SysAdmin job, which involved maintaining Linux-based workloads, while on placement during my master's at an écoles d'ingénieurs, a prestigious form of higher education in France. After my Computer Science degree I moved to London, where I got in on the ground floor with DevOps and cloud engineering.

In London I experienced rapid growth first-hand, which taught me what it takes to create better platforms faster. That’s why I always keep a product mindset, by working to understand your business while always keeping sight of the longer-term. I’m hands-on, meticulous and passionate about the small details. By getting to know every inch of your product, I can adapt my services to your needs.

I love solving problems. I love seeing companies grow. I love supporting businesses and the people who run them, because it’s in my DNA. Most of all, I believe that businesses succeed when their infrastructures are secure, scalable and easy to maintain. With GBergeret Cloud Services, that's exactly what I help my customers achieve.
